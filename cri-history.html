Our vast experience in the development of yoga in the enchanted world of “stories” in schools

Since 2014, we have been in extracurricular activities in schools: day care centers, kindergartens, 1st cycle and secondary level. But it was with the introduction of yoga in the curriculum structure of pre-school education that we could see the best results.

Concomitantly with the need to learn and comply with school curricula, it is important to educate for values ​​and attitudes directed towards life in society considering internal motivation and active and creative participation in each individual learning and respect for the development of Being in an integral way.

Yoga for kids

Yoga for children cultivates and values ​​the fertile ground where all children flourish - action for fun, learning by playing, experimenting and imitating. Yoga classes for children are very fun with games, stories, songs, creative, artistic, but also reflective moments, which stimulate concentration and relaxation. While the postures prepare the body to balance energy, to develop coordination, flexibility, body awareness, breaths work to control emotions, concentration and self-confidence and relaxation allows the child to be empowered with tools to manage creative energy and to enjoy of tranquility.

Yoga Adventures and “Stories”

Yoga allows children to be involved in the story and lead them to experience it through gestural, vocal, musical, motor, emotional, cognitive and social expression. With the experience of the story we can awaken different interests and curiosities of the child, as well as teach him / her reading habits that can have effects on the logical reasoning, in the way they experience their emotions, to work the critical spirit and instill in him / her timeless values . As Carla Maia Gonçalves, writer of books for children and teacher of playoga, mentions, “the tales speak to the emotional ear and help children in their perception and decoding of the outside world”. “In addition to their aesthetic and literary value”, the books carry rich learning and life lessons.
